About Me


Undergraduate Telecommunication Student

I am currently a third-year Telecommunication Engineering student at Institut Teknologi Bandung. I have developed a broad interest in various fields within telecommunication, which makes me feel like "all-rounder." Initially, my background was more focused on informatics, which made it challenging to grasp some of the concepts in telecommunication. However, I had the opportunity to meet inspiring and supportive people who motivated me to persevere.

Now, I am fully focused on completing my degree and am still actively involved in competitive programming, which is closely related to my informatics interests. My team and I had the incredible opportunity to compete in the ICPC Asia-Jakarta Regional Competition in both 2023 and 2024. These experiences reinforced my belief that, no matter where I study, my passion remains unshaken.

One of the most influential parts of my university journey has been my involvement in the IMT "Signum" ITB organization, where I met incredible people who contributed significantly to my personal growth. Being part of this group has continuously pushed me to improve.

If I were to highlight an interesting experience during my studies, it would be the IMT inauguration ceremony, as well as maintaining a 100% attendance record in all my courses during the 4th semester—both of which were key milestones for me.
